Larry's pick

Concept 3 as the primary wordmark (used in header, footer, social previews, email signatures). It's honest — the name IS the brand, you're a founder not an agency.

Paired with a minimal "S." favicon (serif S + rust period) for 16/32px spots. Clean, unique, no competing shape language.

This is what most respected solo builders do (Paul Graham, Patrick Collison, Jason Fried). Your name carries the brand, not a symbol.

Other good pick: Concept 1 if you want something that works as a square avatar (Twitter, Github) without needing the full name. Concept 2 is honestly the weakest — too many horizon logos already exist.
