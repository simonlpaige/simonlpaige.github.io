Our Story

Your Neighborhood Produce Stand Since Day One

Everyday Produce is more than a market — it's a Waldo institution. Owner Konrad and the team hand-pick every piece of fruit, every bunch of greens, and every tomato from KC's best local farmers and trusted distributors.

We believe great produce shouldn't cost a fortune. That's why our loyal customers keep coming back, season after season.
