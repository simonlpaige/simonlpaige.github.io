Common . Questions

The FAQs.

Do I own my website?

Yes. I build it, you own the code. If you ever want to leave, I'll hand you the files and help you set up somewhere else. No lock-in.

How long does it take?

Most sites are live within a week of the first conversation. I'll talk to you for 15 minutes, write the copy with your input, build it, and text you the link.

What does the monthly fee cover?

Hosting, SSL, uptime monitoring, and small updates (changing hours, adding a photo, fixing a typo). You email me, I handle it. You're paying for me to keep it running and respond when something needs to change.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts. If you cancel, your site stays live through the end of the billing period and I'll send you a zip of your files.

What if I already have a website?

I'll look at it, tell you what I'd change and why, and give you an honest opinion on whether it's worth rebuilding. Sometimes the answer is no.

Why not just use Wix or Squarespace?

Those are software you're renting. You pay monthly, you do all the building yourself, and when you stop paying, it disappears. What I build is a plain file on a server. It doesn't break when a platform updates. It works the same way in five years as it does today.

Do you only work with KC businesses?

Mostly, yes. I like meeting the people I build for. But if you're outside KC and the project is a good fit, send me an email and we'll talk.
