// Frequently Asked Questions
How can you charge so much less than agencies?
No office rent, no sales team, no project managers, no overhead. I'm one developer with AI-assisted tooling that lets me build faster than a team of five. You're paying for the work, not the bureaucracy.
Do I own my website?
Yes. You own the design, the content, and the code. If you ever want to leave, I'll hand over everything — no lock-in, no hostage fees.
What if I need changes after launch?
Content updates (hours, photos, text changes) are included free with your monthly hosting. Just text or email me and it gets done, usually same day. Larger feature additions are quoted separately.
Do I need to provide my own photos?
You can, but you don't have to. The Business and Storefront plans include professional photography. For Starter, I can use stock photos or shoot your business for an additional $75.
What about a domain name?
If you already have one, I'll connect it. If you need one, I'll help you pick and register it — typically $12-15/year through a registrar like Namecheap or Google Domains.
Can I see an example?
Sure — check out Everyday Produce Market, a site I built for a local Waldo business. Every project gets the same attention to detail.
How fast can I get my website?
Starter sites: 3 days. Business sites: 5-7 days. Storefront with e-commerce: 7-10 days. That's calendar days, not "business weeks."
